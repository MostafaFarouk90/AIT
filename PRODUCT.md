# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Facilities directors, project consultants, government procurement officers, and building developers in the UAE / MENA evaluating a security-systems integration partner. They arrive from a referral or tender shortlist, usually on desktop at the office, and need to judge credibility, capability breadth, and project track record within a minute. Roughly half read Arabic first.

## Product Purpose

Marketing landing site for AIT — Advanced Integrated Technologies LLC, a Dubai-based security systems and low-current integrator (est. 2014, Port Saeed, Deira). The site must convert visitors into contact inquiries by proving: 12+ years of delivery, four service lines (Security & Low-Current Integration, Datacenter Business Solutions, Network & Security, Tailored Applications), and a reference list including Expo City, Mitsubishi ME HQ, Federal Authority for Government HR, Abu Dhabi University, and dozens of towers, hotels, and communities across the UAE. Fully bilingual English/Arabic with RTL, language auto-detected from the browser.

## Brand Personality

Precise, assured, understated-premium. "The best but not necessarily the largest" (their own vision statement). Feels like a control room run by people who have done this hundreds of times — calm authority, not sales pressure.

## Anti-references

- Navy-blue-and-orange generic security-company template (the current aittechs.com Bootstrap look).
- SaaS gradient-hero clichés, glassmorphism cards, stat-counter heroes.
- Stock handshake/padlock clip-art imagery.

## Design Principles

1. Proof over promise — real project names, real clients, real scope lists do the persuading.
2. The existing brand speaks — deep navy (#1A3E69 family) carries the surfaces, brand cyan (#22a1c4) is the signal color, logo-swoosh orange drives CTAs.
3. Arabic is a first-class citizen — RTL layout, Cairo typeface, native copy, never machine-literal.
4. Motion signals precision — choreographed, exponential-ease reveals; nothing bouncy or decorative.
5. Every fold has one job — hero convinces, services explain, projects prove, contact converts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1, full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives, keyboard-navigable nav and accordion, `lang`/`dir` switched on the root element per language.
